# PinePhone

https://wiki.pine64.org/index.php/PinePhone

## Labels

* AIF1 is the CPU.
* AIF2 is the eg25 modem.
* AIF3 is the cs8723 Bluetooth, currently unsupported.
* Mic1 is the internal mic.
* Mic2 is the headset mic.
* Line Out is the speaker.
